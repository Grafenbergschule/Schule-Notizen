- Health benefits, eating pattern --> reduce stress
- Increase the variety of products you eat + nutrition intake
- reduce the demand of animal based products --> animal protections, ethical reasons
- eat less meat = reduce greenhouse gas emissions
- meat needs more resources than plants (pant based foods)

	- reduce water
	- energy

- meat offers essential nutrients (e.g. iron, proteins)

- meat as a traditional food
- eating meat is our freedom but also our responsibility 
- Banning meat seem impossible

 - personal choice + awareness/education
 - Ethical reasons: us humans have responsibility for animals + the environment
