

### Analysis: Incredible Foods (Nora Gaffney)

**1. Text Function**

- **Persuasive:** To convince a diverse audience (investors to skeptics) to support the company’s plant-based mission.
    
- **Informative:** To explain the environmental benefits of methane reduction and biomass regrowth.
    

**2. Register**

- **Consultative/Semi-formal:** Blends professional business communication with a personal, approachable "human" voice.
    

**3. Text Structure**

- **Introduction:** Emotional hook using the "world as a ship" metaphor.
    
- **Body (Numbered):** Two-part scientific explanation (Methane & Biomass).
    
- **Pivot:** Drastic warning (the "asteroid" metaphor) to create urgency.
    
- **Conclusion:** Transparent business reality and a direct Call to Action (CTA).
    

**4. Tone**

- **Visionary:** Optimistic about nature's recovery.
    
- **Candid/Urgent:** Honest about high costs and the "existential threat" of extinction.
    
- **Colloquial:** Humorous touches (e.g., "cow farts") to keep the reader engaged.
    

**5. Language (Stylistic Devices)**

- **Metaphor:** "Blue, white and green ship" (fragility) vs. "asteroid" (human destruction).
    
- **Inclusive Language:** Constant use of "we" and "our" to build a sense of shared responsibility.
    
- **Hyperbole:** "Clicking our fingers" emphasizes how quickly nature _could_ heal if we change.
    
- **Rhetorical Question:** "Who can argue with a win-win...?" to make the conclusion feel undeniable.
