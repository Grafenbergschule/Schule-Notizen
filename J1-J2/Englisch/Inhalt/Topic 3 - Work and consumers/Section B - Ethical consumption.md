**Conscious consumption:**
a movement that encourages people to be aware of what they buy and to use their money to support brands that have the same values whenever possible

**Ethical consumption:**
Is a type of activism which acknowledges that customers not only consume goods, but also the process used to produce these goods. It is more common than not to discover corporations exploiting their workers or destroying the environment in order to cut costs on a product or service. One way to negate these practices is to buy ethically made products crafted by small businesses and local artisans. By supporting ethically sourced businesses, consumers can help to protect the environment while simultaneously avoiding goods that exploit child workers, slave labor, or natural resources
