1. the consequences of the pandemic for office workers
potential rise in gender inequality due to a permanent switch to working from home
2. concerns about the change for gender equality
Thought that increase in working from home could even out the playing field of men and women; however, women (family carers) may not be able to take advantage of it, as men will show their faces at work more and therefore continue to be at an advantage over women!
3. choices facing workers with caring responsibilities or disabilities 
Carers or people with disabilities will more often choose to stay home; may look like they are less committed to their jobs; less good relationships with their manager, which could affect pay raises and/or promotions
4. the responsibilities of employers towards their staff
companies have to take responsibility for the problems and set up systems that do not discriminate against people who work from home; need for encouragement from senior management to entry-level, regardless of gender, so that all people take advantage of working from home
